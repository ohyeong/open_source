Seoul Amateur Orchestra Club

Seoul is home to numerous amateur orchestras, many of which are operated by universities, and the total number exceeds 20.

This webpage allows you to explore these amateur orchestras all in one place.

You can check the locations of the orchestras on the map to the right. By clicking on a marker, you'll be directed to a detailed page with more information about the selected orchestra.

You can also use the search feature at the bottom to conveniently find the orchestra you're looking for.

This website provides valuable information for music enthusiasts and those interested in amateur orchestral activities in the Seoul area. Discover the history, performance schedules, and ways to participate in various orchestras, and embark on a new musical journey.

Our goal is to connect music lovers and share the charm of amateur orchestras with a wider audience. We warmly welcome your visit and hope you enjoy exploring!
